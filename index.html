Ioannis (Yannis) Mitliagkas

I am a PhD student at The University of Texas at Austin, working with Constantine Caramanis and Sriram Vishwanath.




Research Interests

Machine Learning, High Dimensional Statistics, Large Problems.
I recently worked on streaming algorithms, optimization on graphs. Using platforms for large scale computation like MapReduce and Graphlab/Giraph, I'm working on graph (node centrality) and biomedical applications (drug-drug interactions in the design of cancer cocktails).
