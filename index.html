Ioannis (Yannis) Mitliagkas



  

About me

I am a PhD student at The University of Texas at Austin, working with Constantine Caramanis and Sriram Vishwanath.


Education

Diploma, ECE, Technical University of Crete, Greece 2002-2008.
MSc. in ECE, Technical University of Crete, Greece 2008-2009.
Advised by Prof. Nikos Sidiropoulos


Research Interests

Machine Learning, High Dimensional Statistics, Large Problems.
I recently worked on streaming algorithms, optimization on graphs. Using platforms for large scale computation like MapReduce and SPARK, I'm working on graph (node centrality) and biomedical applications (drug-drug interactions in the design of cancer cocktails).
